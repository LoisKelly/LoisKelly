Biography

I am about to graduate from the UAL Creative Computing Institute, from the MSc Data Science and AI for the Creative Industries. I am passionate about exploring the synergy between AI and the creative industries, particularly with music. I have a degree in Jazz Vocals and have been working as a Data & Logistics Manger at a music PR company for the last 2 years. I am also a lead vocalist and keyboardist in the band Home Counties with our debut album releasing 3rd May 2024. In my free time I enjoy swimming, reading, and playing guitar!
