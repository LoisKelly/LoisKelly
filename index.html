Biography

I am about to graduate from the UAL Creative Computing Institute with an MSc in Data Science and AI for the Creative Industries. I am passionate about exploring the synergy between AI and the creative industries, particularly with music. I hold a BMus in Jazz Vocals from the Guildhall School of Music and Drama and have been working as a Data & Logistics Manager at a music PR company for the last two and a half years. Additionally, I am the lead vocalist and keyboardist in the band Home Counties. Check out our debut album "Exactly as it Seems", released on May 3rd, 2024. In my free time, I enjoy swimming, reading, and playing guitar!
